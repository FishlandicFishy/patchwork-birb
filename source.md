# Summaries of Source Works

## Introduction
This document serves to summarise the gameplay of a portion of works inspiring `patchworkBirb`. These works are not accessible online, and referencing them in `patchworkBirb` without reasonable attribution would be meaningless. This summary aims to provide context for viewers unfamiliar with the original works.

## Disclaimer
Fishy does NOT own any of the works below, except for *Struck by the 'Tism* which Fishy only partially owns.

## *Escape School Obby No Clickbait*
*Escape School Obby No Clickbait* is a survival-based tabletop game debuted in the *Become a Board Game Designer* programme. Its main objective is to escape a school environment.

### Cards
* Draw one item
* Key (can be used to exit the school within 3 rounds; if exceeding 3 rounds, discard)
* Trash (useless)
  * Permanently borrowed as Trash (37a⁻¹)

  * Permanently borrowed as Ball Pen (32a)

* Generic Energy Drink (negates negative effects)
* The School Nurse's All-Mighty Ice Pack (heal)

## *Goblin* (who says "Ni")
*Goblin* (also known as *Goblin Who Says "Ni"*) is a tabletop game debuted in the *Become a Board Game Designer* programme. According to its creators, the game takes place in "Goblin World", and serves to simulate how life is "random", "unfair", and "cruel". It is unclear whether the premise of the game is related to the Monty Python "knights who say 'ni'".

The game has directly influenced the card "*jan soweli* who is only 105 cm tall and can only say “ger river”" (39a).

### Details
* Players: 3 to 6
* Age: Kindergarten to high school

### Cards
* It's time to stop: All action cards ineffective
* The Card's Power: After 2 rounds, if you have no penalty, you will gain one level
* Bye: Tell one player to discard all cards
* Time Machine: Choose any card from the card stack
  * Permanently borrwed as Scavenger (37a)
* Thief: Steal 2 cards from any player
* No Cards?: Tell another player to draw a card
* (can't read): Slowest player draws a card
  * Permanently borrowed as Stand Up. Let’s Have a Greeting (38a)
* Downgrade Card (cat)
* Upgrade Card (is that a campfire)
* Upgrade Card (firefly in a bottle)
* Frost Spell: A player cannot draw any cards
* Detective: If a player looks upward, they lose 3 cards
* Table Span(k?)ing: If a player slaps the table, they lose 3 cards
  * Permanently borrowed as Prosecuted for Damaging Property (1t)
* (lost title): If a player looks at the time, they lose 3 cards
  * Permanently borrowed as What’s the Time? (2t)
* (lost title): If a player wears glasses, they lose 3 cards
  * Permanently borrowed as 🤓 (3t)
* (lost title): If a player sleeps or rests in any way, they lose 3 cards
  * Permanently borrowed as You may not sleep now, there are monsters nearby (4t)

## *Felix's Adventure*
*Felix's Adventure* is a tabletop game about submarines, which debuted in the *Become a Board Game Designer* programme.

The game may have inspired the "Plot" section of `patchworkBirb`.

## *Struck by the 'Tism*
*Struck by the 'Tism*<sup>[unofficial title]</sup> is a tabletop game debuted in the *Become a Board Game Designer* programme. It controversially uses third-party proprietary software in its core design (Nintendon't's fursonas), creating non-free blobs in its firmware.

Fishy has notably participated in its development, but deeply regrets it.

The creators' frequent mispronunciation of "abilities" as "abaties" has inspired the card "Abaties of Characters" (42a).
